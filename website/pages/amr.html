AMR Hackathon 2018 (Heidelberg, Germany)

I was selected to participate in a 3-day AMR Hackathon organized by EIT Health Germany, in Heidelberg (Germany). At the beginning, we were divided in teams of 6/7 elements that would proceed to an AMR analysis for a different EU country.
We were requested to:

  1. Analyze guidelines and regulation (related to AMR) in France;
  2. To verify if the country is following any EU directives;
  3. To find out successful stories and disastrous events that took place in a local or national level;
  4. Finally, we were told to think of an innovative approach to handle AMR.

At the end, we prepared a poster and a 1-slide presentation that were presented to 5 judges with important roles in EIT Health.
